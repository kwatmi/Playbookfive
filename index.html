ABOUT US

Our team came together through a shared interest and passion to fulfill one goal -- Leverage technology to improve the world of sport.

Since our inception we have performed thousands of Virtual Reality sessions with athletes ranging from amateur to professional. We have also worked alongside High School and Collegiate teams to support their needs and test our solutions - including a research project with the Loughborough Sports Tech Institute.

Today, we use the research and experience gathered from working alongside the best players and coaches of American Football to build the future of sport across several sports including Soccer, Hockey, and Volleyball.
